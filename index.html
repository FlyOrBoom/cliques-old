How to play
Cliques is a fun online card game based on the popular game of Set.
The game has 81 cards, each having four features: shape, color, shade, and number. Each of the four features have three varieties. For example, the shape can be a squiggle, a diamond, or a pill. Two cards may share the same variety for a feature, so that feature is "identical" amoung the two cards. Otherwise, the feature "unique" for these two cards. Note that none of the cards have all four of their features sharing the same varieties. You earn points if you find a "clique" — a group of three cards whose symbols must satisfy all of the following criterias:
· Their shapes must all be unique or all be identical.                   
· Their shades must all be unique or all be identical.                   
· Their colors must all be unique or all be identical.                     
· Their amount of shapes must all be unique or all be identical.
